Barbershop FAQ

Expert answers to your questions about our services in the Antelope Valley.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are highly recommended to avoid long wait times. Walk-ins are accepted based on availability.

How long does a haircut take?

A standard haircut typically takes about 30 to 45 minutes. We take our time to ensure a precision cut.

Do you do kids' haircuts?

Yes, we offer haircuts for children aged 12 and under. We make sure they are comfortable and leave looking sharp.

Do you offer straight razor shaves?

Yes, we specialize in traditional hot towel straight razor shaves for the ultimate grooming experience.

What products do you use?

We use premium men's grooming products for hair and beard care. We also have products available for purchase.

Where are you located?

We are centrally located in Palmdale, easily accessible from Lancaster and surrounding areas.
